Week in Review

And so I thought I knew what I was walking into. I thought that there was a product and I thought we'd have something built by October.

People aren't trying to let you down or be unrealistic:

Quote of hey it'll be done by October.

Translation: I'm feeling hopeful about the progress that we're making.

The question isn't how much can you get done now. It's how much can you get done when you aren't sleeping well. And know that you've only got two more weeks of runway left.

So you want to start a startup. I'd do everything you want to do and then see if the feeling is still there. This is a moth to the flame type moment.
