'Raising Angel Funds'

Published: Tue 22 October 2019
By Joe Newbry

In misc.

List of the most useful things you can do to raise angel funds: - Profitable business - 1 or more paying customers or week over week user growth - 1 SAFE note in the bank (round already has momentum)

The best way to raise angel funds is to raise from people that you already know. There is no difference between money and trust. So raising money is a trust building exercise. This is why you don't have you brother sign a legal document when you loan him $5 grand. You trust each other and know the debt will be paid. In a similar light the people who are writing.

Weekly updates and survivability matter.

Start once you've gotten cash from a single person. This makes the process a lot smoother. One part of every fundraising discussion that I'm having is who else have you raised from and when is the roundn going to close. Setting a date and getting one person over the finish line is a big step forward but you don't want to announce fundraising until the process has momentum.
